Maha Ashtami

A soft, early morning light bathed the ancient city of Kathmandu, Nepal, as the golden echoes of bells and chants filled the air. It was the eighth day of the Dasain festival, the most cherished time of the Nepalese calendar, celebrated to honor the victory of the goddess Durga over the buffalo demon, Mahishasura. This day, known as Maha Ashtami, is considered especially potent, when the goddess is believed to attain her ultimate power and strength.
