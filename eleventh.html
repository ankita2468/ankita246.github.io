Papankusha Ekadashi

Papankusha Ekadashi is also called Ashwin Shukla Paksha Ekadashi, based on the exact tithi that it falls on in the Hindu Calendar. The spiritual significance of Papankusha Ekadashi lies in its power to rid one's soul of sins and negative karmas, as the name suggests. Devotees observe a strict fast on this day, refraining from food and water.
